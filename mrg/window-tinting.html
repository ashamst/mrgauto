Car Window Tinting in Dubai

MRG Auto Care is a professional auto tint specialists with a range of highly qualified and experienced staff for all kind of car or suv. MRG Auto Care always rely on the latest technology and equipment for installing windows tinting. Car window tinting in Dubai provides the cost effective method of keeping your car or 4x4 interiors free from the harsh climate effects. We only use branded window tints with variety of shades and our service adviser can assist you in selecting the right material for your car or suv.

The factory glass in most car or suv won’t fully block the sun’s UV rays. Prolonged exposure results in damage to skin and contributes to fading of upholstery, warping or cracking of other interior finishes of the car or suv. We use only window tints which will blocks up to 99% of harmful UV rays, helping to keep you and your passengers protected from sun damage. Window tint provides varying degrees of relief. Ceramic tint is the latest in the market and also help you feel the coolest and can even help to cool the surfaces that are normally too hot to touch. Metallized tint is also effective at giving you a cooler interior. Dyed tint provides heat relief as well, but doesn’t have the added technology that’s found in ceramic or metallized products. Instead, dyed tint helps by providing shade. Keep in mind that with dyed tint, the darker you go, the more cooling power you’ll get.

Tint helps protect your eyes from glare and prevents fatigue, tempering the sun like a pair of top-quality sunglasses. The relief you get helps keep you safer and can make every minute you spend behind the wheel more comfortable too – even if it’s a cloudless day with blazing hot sun. There are a lot of reasons it’s good to have the interior of your vehicle more protected from prying eyes: an expensive sound system, a habit of leaving items in the car overnight, or when you’re parking in an area that’s not lit well.Tint makes it more difficult to see inside your vehicle, helping to disguise potential valuables. Our tint comes in a generous range of charcoal shades to provide varying levels of privacy, from limo-dark to a subtle hint of gray to clear. When you choose your shade, remember to consider privacy level as well as the look of the car or suv .

As much as we hope to avoid them, accidents sometimes happen. It helps to know, every day you’re on the road, that you and your passengers are safer if your auto glass gets broken. When you choose Our tint, you’ll have many years to enjoy your eye-catching custom look. And if you choose a clear tint, you can trust that it will provide the protection you want with a practically invisible appearance.
