Graphene Ceramic Paint Protection in Dubai

MRG Auto Care is a professional Graphene Ceramic Coating specialists with a range of highly qualified and experienced staff for all kind of car or SUV. Graphene Ceramic Coating is a clear, nanocrystalline coating that protects your car or suv, from weather, chemicals, and UV rays while repelling water, dirt and other contaminants. Graphene ceramic coating is having 10H hardness prolong duration of protection even in the harshest conditions and is the latest technology of ceramic coating.

Graphene coatings are ultra-hard and anti-static which when coated on your car or suv and doesn’t allow the dust and dirt to settle down. Graphene coating is the latest revolution in the automobile industry. Graphene containing one layer carbon atoms is the strongest material that exists on the earth.

Benefit of Graphene Ceramic Paint Protection

High water contact angle ie more beading

Highly flexible

Enhanced hydrophobic properties

Less prone to water spotting

Higher stain resistance against bugs, saps, droppings, etc.

Higher scratch resistance during the washing process

Ultrahigh-gloss and color depth

Ease of application

Longer Durability (7-10 years)

Reduction in heat absorption on treated surfaces
