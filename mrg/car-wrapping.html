Car Wrapping

MRG Auto Care is a professional Car wrapping installation specialist in Dubai with a range of highly qualified and experienced staff for all kind of car or SUV. Car wraps always create a distinctive or individual cool look on any car or SUV. They come in different colors like carbon fiber looks, brushed metal, matte colors, and high gloss colors. The vinyl used in designing the wrap is a preferred option because it is lighter than paint.

Benefits of Car Wrapping

Car wrapping is an ideal car surface protection solution for your vehicle, especially for Dubai Deserts. This becomes quite essential when you own a car or SUV in Dubai since it is cost-effective, long lasting, safe, and convenient solution for preserving car color, surface, and condition. It can also be removed easily without causing any damage to the car exterior.

Most Common Questions
How long do car wraps last?
Car wraps can last based on the manufactures warranty. There is no definite answer about the longevity of a car wrap, it typically depends on the car or suv usage, and how much it is exposed to factors such as sunlight, car wash frequency, dust and pollution, and other factors. If your car is out in the sun for longer periods of time then the durability of the wrap will be shorter.
How long does the Car Wrapping installation take?
The full Car wrapping installation takes around 5-6 working days, this includes preparing the car, paint correction, car wrapping installation, curing and inspection.
How do I wash my car after Car wrapping application?
Any soap is fine to wash your car or SUV but we would recommend manual wash only.
How often we need to do the maintenance of the Car Wrapping application?
We ask our customer to come back after 1 month for a check up after Car wrapping application. After this time, there is need for a yearly check up/maintenance in our MRG Auto Care studio.
Can you apply Ceramic coating on the top of Car wrapping?
Yes, most of our customer opt for this as it really helps to give a hydrophobic effect for easier cleaning.
