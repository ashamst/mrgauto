MRG Auto Garage

MRG Auto Garage (Motor Racing Garage) is run by a team of dedicated automotive industry professionals committed to providing high standards in repair and service. We service your car by the manufacturer’s schedules and comply with the standard servicing requirements for manufacturers’ guarantees and extended warranties.


We offer an efficient and professional vehicle repair service for all types of motor vehicles. We specialize in the general repairs of cars, periodic servicing, electrical systems, air conditioning service and repairs, electronic diagnostics for engine, interior, and exterior body works, and custom modification of vehicles. We are a leading car repair garage and auto service workshop in Dubai offering tire repair, brake repair, car ac repair, mechanical services, body and dent services.

MRG Auto Garage is one of the most trusted Off-Road Accessories and Modification Garage in Dubai. We are specialized in customizing off-road vehicles to suit the offroad needs of the customer. When it comes to customizing your car or 4x4, MRG Auto Garage is the final destination for it.


We offer competitive prices on vehicle repair, maintenance, denting, painting, accident repairs, vehicle modifications, and quick lube services. We have an experienced after-sales service team for your support. We provide 24X7 emergency road services from our service centre. MRG Auto Care

MRG Auto Care is a professional auto detailing specialist with a range of highly qualified and experienced staff. We provide Top Quality Car Detailing services that will give your Car or 4x4 a showroom finish. Our services include:

  • New car protection.
  • Ceramic paint protection.
  • Machine polishing
  • Stone chip protection
  • Suspension check
  • Deep interior cleaning and polishing.

We are specialized in paint correction, ceramic coatings, and the installation of PPF (paint protection film). We also provide compound polishing, dents removal, and paint glazing.


MRG Auto Care relies on the latest equipment for our interior and exterior Car Detailing and use high-quality body treatment products for detailing & polishing to produce a high gloss finish. Our team has extensive experience working with all types of SEDAN and SUV cars like Audi, Toyota, Lexus, Porsche, Mercedes, Rolls Royce, Nissan, Aston Martin, Tesla, and Range Rover to restore the new look of your Car.
