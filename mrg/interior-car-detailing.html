Interior Car Detailing

MRG Auto Care is a professional auto detailing specialist with a range of highly qualified and experienced staff. We provide Top Quality interior Car Detailing services in Dubai that will give your car or suv a better finish. MRG Auto Care relies on the latest equipment for our interior and exterior car Detailing and use high-quality body treatment products for detailing & polishing to produce a high gloss finish. Our team has extensive experience working with all types of SEDAN, and SUV cars like Audi, Toyota, Lexus, Porsche, Mercedes, Rolls Royce, Nissan, Aston Martin, Tesla and Range Rover restore the new look of your car.

Interior Car Detailing Services Include
  • Complete deep cleaning of seats, carpets, mats, doors, dashboard console and headliner
  • Leather or fabric seat cleaning with conditioning, shampoo upholstery, carpet and floor mats
  • Cleaning all surfaces using compressed air, fine brushes, soft cloths and specially formulated cleaners
  • Cleaning AC vents, console, seat, belts, etc.
  • Cleaning of all windows inside and outside and remove water spots.
  • Interior polish and stain protection
  • Hand wash & dry
  • Interior Disinfection, Sanitization
  • Leather and Fabric Protection

Our detailing experts use specialized cleaners and conditioners that offers complete protection for the Leather and fabric seat or surfaces of the car. We aim to serve you to achieve ultimate vehicle protection by using the right products and tools. We assist in protecting your car’s interior by ensuring it receives proper care and attention. Our interior leather and fabric protection service include protection from UV rays, help prevent fading and enhance the look and feel of your car’s interior.
