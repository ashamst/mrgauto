Ceramic Paint

MRG Auto Care is the best professional auto ceramic specialist in Dubai and a Certified Installer of System X Ceramic Paint Protection Coating in the Dubai. System X Ceramic Coating is a high-performance 9H nano ceramic coating applied on the vehicle’s exterior surface. System X offers exceptional protection and is extremely durable and robust, providing high chemical, UV and heat resistance. It creates an excellent clear coat over the vehicle’s paint, increases its hardness and leaves a fantastic deep gloss over the original clear coat.

After the ceramic coating is applied, it forms a permanent bond within the paint and offers protection from all types of scratches and swirl marks. System X Ceramic Coating is Boeing certified which makes this product effective for aeronautic use.

MRG Auto Care services include new car protection, ceramic paint protection, machine polishing, stone chip protection and deep interior and exterior cleaning and polishing. We are specialized in paint correction, ceramic coatings and the installation of PPF (paint protection film). We also provide compound polishing, dent removal and paint glazing.

Our team has extensive experience working with all types of SEDAN, and SUV cars like Audi, Toyota, Lexus, Porsche, Mercedes, Rolls Royce, Nissan, Aston Martin, Tesla and Range Rover. We make your car shine and restore its the new look. We deliver the best ceramic coating service and experience for our customers. Our team of trained specialists provide a world-class service that suits your needs.
