RTA Vehicle Inspection in Dubai

MRG Auto Garage offers a pre-RTA vehicle inspection service that checks the condition of your vehicle or car or 4x4, identifies and rectify the defects that would assist you in the RTA inspection. MRG Auto Garage offers service to pick up the vehicle from your home, inspect it at the workshop to make sure the vehicle defects are rectified, take the car to the RTA for passing and helps to do the registration via online or RTA apps. MRG Auto Garage provides RTA vehicle inspections in Dubai, located in Al Quoz Industrial Area. MRG Auto Garage assures you the best service at highly competitive price.

The Inspection Includes

01

Vehicle Identification Check includes registration plate check and chassis number verification

02

Exhaust Emissions Check

03

Braking System Check, which includes Service Brake Mechanical condition and operation. This also includes the Parking brake performance and efficiency

04

Steering and steering wheel check which include Mechanical Condition, Steering Play, Steering system attachment and coupling

05

Visibility Check, which includes Field of vision, condition of glass, Rear‐view mirrors, Windscreen wipers, Screen washers

06

Lighting equipment Check which includes checking the main and dipped‐beam headlamps, condition and operation, condition of lenses, colour and visual efficiency

07

Another equipment checking, which includes checking the Mounting of driver's seat, Mounting of battery, Audible warning device, Warning triangle, Safety belts etc

Vehicle Testing Centers in Dubai

New car or 4x4 vehicles are exempted from three years of annual vehicle inspections if you are the car's first owner. Vehicle testing details will be received from the RTA websites or call centres.

Documents required for Registration Renewal
  • Pass certificate from the RTA certified testing centres.
  • UAE nationals, or GCC nationals, or Residency in UAE.
  • Vehicle Insurance.
